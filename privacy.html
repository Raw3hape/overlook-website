Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Information We Collect

Overlook is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

Photos and Videos

All photos captured with Overlook are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your photos unless you explicitly choose to share them for AI analysis.

AI Analysis Data

When you use the AI photo analysis feature, your photo is temporarily sent to our secure servers for processing. Photos are immediately deleted after analysis and are never stored on our servers.

Device Information

We collect basic device information (iOS version, device model) solely for app compatibility and crash reporting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Photo Analysis

Photos sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We do not store, share, or use your photos for any other purpose.

App Improvement

Anonymous crash reports and usage statistics help us improve the app experience.

Data Storage and Security

  • All photos are stored locally on your device
  • Photos sent for analysis use end-to-end encryption
  • We do not store any photos on our servers
  • Analysis results are stored locally on your device

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

OpenAI API

For photo analysis. Photos are processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy and are not used for training their models.

Vercel

For secure API hosting. They process data according to their privacy policy.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete any photo from the app at any time
  • Disable AI analysis features
  • All data is stored locally and can be removed by deleting the app
  • No account or personal information is required to use the app

Children's Privacy

Overlook does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general photography use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at:

support@overlook.app
